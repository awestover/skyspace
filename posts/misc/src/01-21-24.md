Nifty LaTeX package.
{body}

This package is very helpful when writing math papers.

```latex
\usepackage{thmtools}
\usepackage{thm-restate}

%% usage of thm-restate:
\begin{restatable}{thm}{thm:goldbach}
\end{restatable}

Later we recall the theorem: \cref{thm:goldbach}

\goldbach*
```
