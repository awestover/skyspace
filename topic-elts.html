overlap of simply periodic binary functions

[05-29-23]

Let $f_a(x)$ denote a binary string consisting of $a$ $0$'s followed by $a$ $1$'s and then repeating these $2a$ symbols. We can equivalently describe $f_a$ as $f_a(x)\defeq \floor{x/a}\bmod 2$. We study $\sum_{x\in [ab]} f_a(x)\oplus f_b(x)$, in particular bounding the difference of the sum from $\frac{ab}{2}$.
